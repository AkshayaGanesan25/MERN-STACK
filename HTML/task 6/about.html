About us

Hyundai is a global automobile manufacturer, founded in South Korea, that has grown into one of the world's largest. The company is known for its diverse range of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, and electric cars, and is also developing technologies like autonomous driving and hydrogen-powered vehicles. Hyundai Motor India is a subsidiary and is positioned as a key export hub for the company.
